# MULTIPLY

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** March 10, 2025

## Content

The article explores "multiply" as a divine principle woven throughout biblical history, beginning with creation and continuing through Jesus's ministry.

God embedded multiplication into creation itself. References Genesis 1:27-30: God told humanity "Be fruitful and multiply. Fill the earth and govern it."

### Biblical Progression
- **Abraham's Legacy:** God promised Abraham his descendants would bless all families on earth, establishing multiplication as a spiritual inheritance
- **Jesus's Example:** Jesus demonstrated multiplication through calling twelve disciples, performing miracles of abundance, and commissioning followers to spread the gospel
- **Disciples' Mission:** After the resurrection, followers received the Holy Spirit and became witnesses "to the ends of the earth"

Multiplication is not optional but a foundational spiritual principle passed from generation to generation. This divine mandate persists despite human failings and continues shaping believers' purpose today.
