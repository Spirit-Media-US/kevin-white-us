# FIGHT!

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** June 20, 2024

## Content

Kevin White discusses spiritual fighting in both personal and professional contexts. His company is "fighting to: Find the right people. Leverage tools like Amazon, Google, YouTube for the glory of God. Be effective. Be profitable."

Spiritual battles are not physical conflicts between people, but internal struggles against evil. References 1 Timothy 6:12: engage in "the good fight for the true faith."

### Five Principles
Truth, righteousness, peace, faith, and salvation.

### Three Weapons
God's Word, prayer in the Spirit, and persistence.

"I want to encourage you to be a FIGHTER. Let God FIGHT through you. The battle belongs to the Lord. The battle has already been won!"
