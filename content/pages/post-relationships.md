# RELATIONSHIPS

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** June 20, 2024

## Content

Business success depends on strong relationships and results. White establishes this through biblical foundations, particularly Matthew 22:36-40, where Jesus emphasizes loving God and others as the greatest commandments.

### God's Model for Relationships
God demonstrates the supreme importance of relationships by becoming human. John 1:14: "The Word became human and made his home among us."

### Business Application
At Spirit Media, White's company prioritizes understanding clients by studying them, representing them authentically, and knowing their audiences. "The most beloved action in any relationship is to be heard."

### Practical Guidance
Adopt the Golden Rule (Matthew 7:12) and become a "client in your own company" by genuinely listening to customer needs rather than speaking only to them.

### Listening as Leadership
God listens to human voices. Leaders should identify with client problems and let clients determine when relationships are healthy.
