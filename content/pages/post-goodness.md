# GOODNESS

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** March 19, 2025

## Content

Kevin White shares four daily affirmations centered on God's goodness:
1. God, you are GOOD
2. You are GOOD to me
3. You are GOOD at being God
4. God is GOOD and does GOOD

The post emphasizes recognizing God's goodness rather than focusing solely on material supply. White quotes Steven Furtick: "Perhaps you don't have a supply problem, you have a sight problem."

Key themes include:
- Seeing God's goodness through Scripture (Psalms, Romans, Jeremiah)
- God's intentional plans for good outcomes in believers' lives
- Practical steps to preserve awareness of goodness: search, remember, count, record, protect, proclaim, and share it
- Transformation through goodness: God's kindness convicts, purifies, cleanses, and transforms people

White invites readers to commit to recognizing God's goodness consistently. He hosts "The Kevin White Show" on YouTube every Tuesday at 10 AM.
