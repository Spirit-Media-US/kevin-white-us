# PATHS

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** February 10, 2025

## Content

Kevin White discusses God's guidance through established standards and pathways. He references David's words: "He renews my strength. He guides me along right PATHS, bringing honor to his name" (Psalms 23:3, NLT).

### Six Standards for Spirit Media
1. Know and live out God's Word
2. Pray with Jesus' authority for His Kingdom to come
3. Praise Him at all times
4. Boast only on the Lord
5. Pursue every sales lead He brings
6. Identify, address, and eliminate waste

He warns against a recurring biblical pattern: people become desperate for God, receive blessings, stop depending on Him, and eventually rebel — a cycle that repeats throughout Scripture.

"Purity Proceeds Prosperity" — connecting God's kingdom principles to multiplication and stewardship. PATHS protect, guide, and provide for believers. "God's guidance is God's provision."

Encourages passing these standards to future generations, maintaining clarity around both the "what" and "why" of organizational values.
