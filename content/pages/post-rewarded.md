# REWARDED

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** February 18, 2025

## Content

Kevin White explores the biblical concept of God's rewards for faithful followers. He emphasizes that God fulfills His promises, citing passages from 2 Chronicles and Hebrews.

Most devoted Christians don't genuinely anticipate God's rewards, yet Scripture clearly promises them. "If God says he will reward us for our faith then God will reward us!"

Rewards ultimately belong to Jesus rather than individuals. Believers function as stewards who multiply kingdom impact. When the Father rewards His Son through us, we respond with "worship and gratitude."

### Personal Testimony
At his high school awards ceremony, Kevin's name was unexpectedly called three times — for $1,500, $3,000, and $4,000 scholarships — totaling his first year's tuition. Though neither parent attended college, Kevin became the only sibling to do so, viewing this as divine affirmation of his faith commitment.

White encourages readers to expect God's rewards without dictating how or when they arrive.
