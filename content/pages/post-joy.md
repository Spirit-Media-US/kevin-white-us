# JOY

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** February 24, 2025

## Content

The post explores joy through a biblical lens, identifying three distinct types:
1. **Emotional** — an immediate feeling state
2. **Positional** — a lasting character trait
3. **Spiritual** — fruit of the Holy Spirit

### Language Origins
In Hebrew, *sason* describes sudden exuberance (like a game-winning moment), while in Greek, *simcha* represents sustained joy connected to purpose and shared community experience.

### God's Expectation
Serving God without joy displeases Him — citing Deuteronomy 28:47-48 as warning against joyless service.

### Active Expression
"Rejoice" functions as the active demonstration of joy's internal position. Jesus reportedly used joy-centered greetings rather than standard salutations.

Joy is an unshakeable divine gift independent of personal circumstances, transformative for spiritual practice and daily living. Supported by Luke 2:10 and John 15:11.
