# FAITHFULNESS

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** March 19, 2025

## Content

Kevin White shares a message about God's faithfulness and encourages modeling that same faithfulness through three commitments:

### Praise
Continuous praise should override negative emotions like worry, doubt, and fear. References Psalms 34:1-2, encouraging constant expression of gratitude.

### Prayer
Described as the "information technology system" for business success. Prayer is the essential connection between God's kingdom and earthly operations. "Prayer up – company up. Prayer down – company down."

### Sales
Reframes sales as a spiritual calling rather than purely commercial activity. Sharing God's message and supporting organizations (churches, nonprofits, businesses) all require sales efforts.

White forecasts coming abundance but warns against allowing prosperity to diminish commitment to these three practices. He emphasizes character development and unwavering dedication to kingdom impact.
