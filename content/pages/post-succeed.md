# SUCCEED!

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** June 20, 2024

## Content

Kevin White presents a principle for success: helping others succeed ensures your own success.

Quotes Jeremiah 29:7: "the welfare of the city will determine your welfare." Extends this to company loyalty — employees should pray for leadership and organizational success, as individual prosperity depends on the institution's welfare.

### The Ten Commandments Framework
References Exodus 20:1-17. The Ten Commandments reveal that genuine success requires consideration beyond oneself.

### Business Application
At Spirit Media, White calls his team to embrace loyalty, shared vision, honesty, and waste elimination. References Coca-Cola Consolidated's mission emphasizing God, service, excellence, and profitability — an integrated approach rather than profit-only focus.

White invites Spirit Media employees toward ownership mentality, asserting mutual interdependence: client success enables company success, which enables employee success.
