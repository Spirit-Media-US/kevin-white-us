# BOASTING

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** June 20, 2024

## Content

Kevin White explores the biblical concept of boasting, arguing that all possessions and accomplishments are divine gifts rather than personal achievements.

Opens with 1 Corinthians 4:7 (NLT): "What do you have that God hasn't GIVEN you? And if everything you have is from God, why BOAST as though it were not a GIFT?"

Nothing is owned outright — everything originates from God through Jesus. Possessions, jobs, success, and projects should not fuel personal pride since they represent unearned blessings.

### The Gideon Account (Judges 7:2-24)
God deliberately reduced Gideon's army from 32,000 to 300 soldiers. The reduction prevented the Israelites from attributing victory to their military strength rather than recognizing divine intervention. Despite overwhelming numerical disadvantage, the smaller force prevailed through God's power.

### Key Principles
God multiplies through subtraction: pruning, fasting, tithing, and worship. References Matthew 6:33 and John 12:24 — seeking God's kingdom requires personal denial.

Advocates boasting solely in the Lord (Psalm 34:2, 1 Corinthians 1:29-31). Promotes his book "Gifts."
