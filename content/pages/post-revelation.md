# REVELATION

**Author:** Kevin White
**Date:** January 31, 2025

## Content

Kevin White shares his personal journey with understanding biblical revelation. He recalls childhood fear of the Book of Revelation, but after praying at 17 — "God if there is more to you than I realize, I want to know it" — his perspective transformed fundamentally.

### Key Biblical References
1 Corinthians 2:10-16, Hebrews 1:1-4, and Acts 2:17-18 support how the Divine Spirit communicates spiritual truths to believers.

### What God Reveals
Identity, purpose, calling, strategy, freedom, and guidance for daily decisions.

### Reception Methods
Revelation can arrive through worship, scripture study, prayer, dreams, conversations, and unexpected moments.

### Ground Rules
- Personal revelation precedes revelation for others
- All revelation points toward God
- Authentic revelation aligns with Scripture
